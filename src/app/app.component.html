PeeMap

PeeMap is a user-friendly map app designed to help individuals find nearby toilets. Upon logging in, users see a map marked with toilet locations. They can add new spots, view and rate the cleanliness of existing ones, and get walking directions to the nearest restroom. The app ensures verified access, adapts to different screen sizes, and offers guidance through an info guide. It's a handy tool for those on the go, ensuring they're never far from a restroom.

StarWars

Starship Explorer is a vibrant and immersive platform that takes you on a journey through the universe, unveiling intricate details of starships, their pilots, and their appearances in films. With its visually rich interface, users can delve deep into each starship's lore, see images of iconic pilots, and explore their favorite films. The app is optimized for all devices, ensuring a seamless experience. It's not just an app, but a universe waiting to be explored. Dive in and uncover the cosmic tales of starships and their legends!
